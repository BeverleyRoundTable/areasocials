The Beverley Bounty

1. The Bank: Beverley RT holds all the stickers. To get on the board, you must challenge a host.

  • Bronze (1 pt): Valiant effort (lose to a host).
  • Silver (2 pts): Giant killer (beat a host).
  • Gold (3 pts): The Humiliator (an absolute thrashing / flawless victory).

2. The "Pink Slip" Wager: Guest vs. Guest matches don't generate new points. You must agree to wager your existing stickers (e.g., Bronze for Bronze). The winner physically peels it off the loser.
